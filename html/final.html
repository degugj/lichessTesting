Video Demo

Quick demo of our system with a Fool's mate.

Making Movement

Gantry Control PCB (Lower Left) along with H-Bridge (Lower Right). Seen behind are the DM320T Stepper Motor Controllers. Also in the background: Sensor PCBs, Stepper motor and Electromanget.

Detecting Movement

Raspberry Pi 3 (Lower Left) along with Fast Scanning PCB (Lower Right). Power Distrubution PCB seen in the background.

Final System

As for the final system, we ended up using an aluminium extrusion frame with acrylic surface. The PCBs for hall sensors can be seen under the chess pieces. The display sits in the middle and the power supply can be seen to the right of the display.
